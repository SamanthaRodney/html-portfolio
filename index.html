Hi, I'm Samantha

I’m a creative designer with over 8 years of freelance experience in logo and brand identity design. I love helping businesses bring their vision to life through clean, bold visuals that truly connect with their audience. With 10+ years in corporate communications and project coordination, I combine creative skill with practical expertise. I also design and build websites in WordPress, focusing on usability and style. Alongside my design work, I manage a catering business, which has sharpened my skills in business management and client relations. My approach is hands-on, thoughtful, and results-driven.

What I Do

I work across brand development, website design, print and media design, as well as administrative support, content creation, and IT support. My experience spans branding for catering companies, health and wellness, fitness, cleaning services, community initiatives, and charities. Most recently, I’ve delivered projects for caring and domestic services—providing tailored solutions that help businesses and organisations communicate clearly and connect with their audiences. These are projects I’m proud of, each reflecting a hands-on approach and a strong connection to the client’s goals.
